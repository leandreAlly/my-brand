Coding Is My Passion

I believe that naturally I am inclined to like breaking things down into components and abstract concepts - what is a building? On one hand it’s a collection of building materials arranged in a specific manner, or a series of rooms, usually connected by doors or halls that is used to enclose some space and provide shelter. Programming’a all about this type of thinking - it makes you break down problems, even day to day occurrences, into their atomic components. It forces you to see the moving parts in everything. I believe some people are naturally inclined to like thinking this way. I, for one, absolutely love this pattern of thinking and I want to use it as much as possible. So I build architectures, I break down problems, and I optimise systems. It’s what I love the most, and programming is a fantastic outlet for that.
